Chicken

Chicken Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of the Indian subcontinent. It is made with Indian spices, rice, and usually some type of meat (chicken, beef, goat, lamb, prawn, and fish), or in some cases without any meat, and sometimes, in addition, eggs and potatoes. Biryani is one of the most popular dishes in South Asia, as well as among the diaspora from the region. The dish is prepared with a variety of spices, notably saffron, as well as yogurt; the chicken is marinated in a mixture of yogurt and spices before cooking

Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice (鮨飯, sushi-meshi), usually with some sugar and salt, accompanied by a variety of ingredients (ねた, neta), such as seafood, often raw, and vegetables. Sushi is often served with pickled ginger, wasabi, and soy sauce. There are many different types of sushi, including nigiri sushi, maki sushi, and temaki sushi. Nigiri sushi is a hand-pressed sushi that consists of a small ball of rice with a slice of raw fish on top. Maki sushi is a type of sushi that is made by rolling sushi rice and other ingredients in a sheet of nori seaweed. Temaki sushi is a type of sushi that is made by rolling sushi rice and other ingredients in a cone-shaped piece of nori.
